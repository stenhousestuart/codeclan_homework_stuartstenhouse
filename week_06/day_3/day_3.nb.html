Q3.

Compute and visualise a bootstrap sampling distribution for the mean(lot_area) of the sold houses.


ames_infer_resample_2930 <- ames %>%
  specify(response = lot_area) %>%
  generate(reps = 1000, type = "bootstrap") %>%
  calculate(stat = "mean")
